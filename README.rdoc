== FATTYFITCLUB

Fitness Competition that is team-based.  Teams with highest weight and body-fat
percentage lost wins.  Weekly weigh-ins are informal (metrics don't count), but
are required to stimulate participation. 



* Ruby version: ruby 2.0.0p247

* Classes
  - Contest
  - Team
  - Member
  - Weigh_in

* Postgres

* Testing
  - rspec
  - capybara
  - selenium-webdriver
  - database_cleaner
